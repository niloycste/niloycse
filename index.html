About Me

Hello! I’m M. Mohaiminul Islam, aka Niloy, a dedicated computer science enthusiast. I earned my Bachelor's from the Department of Computer Science & Telecommunication Engineering at Noakhali Science & Technology University, Bangladesh, and an Master's from the Department of Computer Science at SAU, New Delhi. My journey spans diverse projects in Machine Learning, Natural Language Processing, Data Science, and emerging fields like Large Language Modeling, Multimodal LLM and Computer Vision, powered by tools like PyTorch, TensorFlow, and Pandas. I’m driven by curiosity and a passion for impactful innovation.

Apart from my tech-savvy nature, I have a deep fascination with theology, philosophy, and history. My historical interests include Indian medieval history (inspired by Abraham Eraly and Richard Eaton), Bengali history (notably Niharranjan Ray), and Islamic history (with key influences from Karen Armstrong, Lesley Hazleton). In philosophy, I am deeply inspired by Franz Kafka and Friedrich Nietzsche, whose perspectives on existentialism, power, and human nature shape my worldview. I believe that the intersection of technology, history, and philosophy fosters a deeper understanding of knowledge and innovation.
